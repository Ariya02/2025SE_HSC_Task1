Privacy Policy

Effective date: 01/02/25

Welcome to DevNotes. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app, which allows software developers to publish and share their project progress with their team. Security and privacy are of the utmost importance to us.

By using our app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use the app.

  1. Information you provide
    • Developer ID

      • Each user is assigned a unique Developer ID for identification within the app.

    • Profile Information

      • Information such as your name, email address, and other details you choose to provide.

    • Project Information

      • Information related to your project progress, updates, and any other content you publish.

  2. Automatically collected information
    • Usage Data

      • Details about how you use the app, such as timestamps of activities and feature interactions.

    • Device Information

      • Information about the device you use to access the app, including operating system, device type, and app version.

  3. How we use your information

    4. We use the information collected to:

    • Provide and improve the app’s functionality.
    • Facilitate communication between team members.
    • Ensure the security and integrity of your data.
    • Respond to support requests or inquiries.
  4. How We Protect Your Information
    • Encryption

      All project data and sensitive information are encrypted before being stored in our secure servers.

    • Access Control

      Only authorized personnel have access to your data, and strict access controls are in place.

    • Regular Security Audits

      We perform regular security audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

  5. Sharing Your Information

    6. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. Your information may be shared only in the following situations:

    • With Your Team

      Project progress and updates you choose to share are visible to your assigned team members.

    • Legal Compliance

      We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.

  6. Management of Data

    7. You have the right to manage your own data and decide how your data can be used, modified and distributed. These rights include but are not limited to:

    • Access and update your profile information.
    • Delete or modify project data you have published.
    • Deactivate your Developer ID and account upon request.

    If you have any questions or concerns about your data, please contact us at devNotes@gmail.com

  7. Data Retention

    8. We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the app’s services and comply with legal obligations. Upon account deactivation, your data will be securely deleted.

  8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    9. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through the app, and the updated policy will include the effective date.

  9. Contact Us

    10. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

    Email: devNotes@gmail.com

    Thank you for trusting devNotes. Your privacy and security are our priority.
